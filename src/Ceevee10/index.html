KeepUp

KeepUp is an app designed for owners of transportation companies to allow them to KeepUp with the maintenance issues of the cars their employers are allowed to keep in their possessions, so routine maintenance issues won't become costly emergency repairs. The drivers are able to create tickets when any maintenance issue arises, which are sent to the administrators. Upon receiving the ticket, the administrator is able to schedule the repair and assign the driver another car. The application is aimed to give the owner an overview of the status of all their cars, which will allow him/her to forecast expenses and lower operating expenses.
